Sheepy!

A Cute Platformer, from Eksperimental Games

In this short, child-friendly platformer, you play as Sheepy, a young lamb who has gotten separated from his flock. Travel across prairies and through mountains to get home, and back to your family.

There are mud slimes and hay slimes to avoid or fight, and rainbows to collect. It's possible to get through both levels without fighting.

Sheepy! is free to download, and there are no in-game transactions.

If you're playing on Steam, there are two achievements possible: one to collect all the rainbows, and one to not kill any slimes.

See if you can figure out how to tell when a slime is nearby. There's a "subtle" sign post.

There are lots of caves to explore and steep cliffs, because the gameplay was inspired by ibexes leaping across steep mountains and climbing almost-shear dam faces. Sheepy can wall-jump, and double-jump, and slide down cliffs. He's a very bouncy sheep.

Watch for sheep-dogs. When you see them, you'll know Sheepy is getting close to home.
